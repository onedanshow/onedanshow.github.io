Production Management Tools

Production coordinators want to know that a project is on schedule. Here are a few of the projects that I spearheaded while working on the web team for Reel FX Creative Studios:

  • Concept Artwork Manager - App for batch review and categorization of pre-viz & concept artwork
  • Shot Quota Tool - Interactive console for managing artist and department quotas
  • Shot Assignment Tool - Drag-and-drop interface for assigning shots to artists (shown below)

Rails, Backbone JS, MySQL, Mongo, etc.

Reel FX Creative Studios - Circa 2008-2011

Animation Review Tools

Animation supervisors want to give animators great feedback. Here are a few of the applications I made while at Reel FX Creative Studios:

  • Animation Review Tool - App to cue up an animator's work, watch and seek frame-by-frame, draw annotations, and record these along side a supervisor's webcam feedback.
  • High-Rex Artwork Review Tool - App for quickly loading, flipping though, zooming in/out, and commenting on high resolution artwork.

Flash, Flex, PureMVC, Flash Interactive Server

Reel FX Creative Studios - Circa 2008-2011

Hickory Dickory Dock

The assignment was to produce an adaptation of a nursery rhyme. I dreamt, modeled, animated, and produced a one-minute twist of "Hickory Dickery Dock" without the traditional mouse or clock. Made over a semester, I formally learned how to use Maya (after a few years of Blender 3D).

Maya 8, Premiere Pro

Introduction to Animation class - Texas A&M University - Circa 2008

iCanDraw?

For my Masters of Science in Computer Science, I created a proof-of-concept methodology for a computer to provide direction and feedback for assisting a user in learning to draw a human face as accurately as possible.

  • CHI 2010 Paper
  • UIST 2009 Poster

Java, SRL Libraries, Wacom Tablet

Sketch Recognitin Lab - Texas A&M University - Circa 2008-2009

Particle Simulation

I created a flocking simulation to animate particles in a fluid-like motion. The particle flock around targets following a hidden Catmull spline. Several parameters were added to stylize the look, including color and size.

C++, OpenGL, GLUT

Physically Based Modeling class - Texas A&M University - Circa 2007

The Danger of Dangerous Things

Three classmates and I took a short story and adapted it into a short film of the same title. The semester-long process included development meetings, storyboards, scripts, location scouting, casting, shooting schedules, recording of voiceovers, post-production, and more.

Adobe Premiere, Canon XL2

Adaptation for the Short Film class - Auburn University - Circa 2006

Introduction to Computing

A video and web presentation for all new students attending Auburn University's Camp War Eagle orientation. Created in summer 2004 and revised/reshot in spring 2005, I casted, filmed the personalities, and put motion graphics with their words. Using Flash, I built a custom player, complete with closed captioning.

Premiere, Affect Effects, Maya, Photoshop, Canon XL1, Flash

Office of Information Technology - Auburn University - Circa 2004-2005
